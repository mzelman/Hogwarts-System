Hogwarts Student Management System

To add or delete students, choose the "Houses" tab from the top navigation bar, then select a House.
To manage courses and grades, choose the "Courses" tab.
Enrolling students in courses can be done on the student page or in the course page.
Deleting students from a course is only allowed on the course page.
